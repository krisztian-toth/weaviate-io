## Preparation

- Make sure that you have your WCS login details handy. 
- We offer WCS instances at different service level access (SLA) tiers. You can review the [pricing here](../pricing.mdx) and select one that suits you best.

## Instance creation steps

1. Visit the [WCS dashboard](https://console.weaviate.cloud/dashboard) under your WCS account.
1. Select your preferred SLA tier.
1. Enter a unique instance name (this is **not** the same as your instance URL).
1. Select (if applicable):
    1. Whether to enable authentication, and
    1. The host region.
1. Lastly, when you are ready - click "Create" to create your cluster.
